How submissions are recorded

Submissions are recorded automatically when you open a Pull Request on GitHub from your track2/<your-name> branch into dkirsh:main. The PR creation timestamp becomes the “Submitted at” entry; the grade and critique link appear after the instructor finishes reviewing.

Contract bonus. Tasks 2 and 3 each award up to 20 bonus points for contract quality — the rubric’s contract gate must pass first, and the bonus is added on top of the rubric score. Both numbers appear in the Grade column once posted.

What “graded” means here

Each task is graded against the rubric on its own page, with the contract gate as a hard prerequisite. Numerical scores reflect the rubric line items; the comments/critique link points at the PR conversation where the instructor records what worked, what needs revision, and what the work taught us about the AI-assisted process.
