Tuesday · Day 11 · Sprint 2 Launch

Sprint 2 Launch

You have completed your first sprint, demonstrated your work to the class, and learned from both your successes and your failures. Sprint 2 begins today with contract review and advanced techniques for directing AI systems. The scope is larger, the requirements are more demanding, and the integration points with other tracks are more complex. You will apply the lessons from Sprint 1 to build something more sophisticated.

1

Sprint 2 Contract Review

You submitted your Sprint 2 contracts on Monday. Today Prof Kirsh reviews them and provides feedback. The contracts are examined for scope — is this too much work for two weeks, or too little? For acceptance criteria — are the criteria testable and do they match what the system needs? For dependencies — does your Sprint 2 integrate with output from other tracks, and if so, are those contracts coordinated? When your contract is approved, you move forward. If it needs revision, you have until the end of the day to submit a revised version.

2

Advanced AI Direction: Progressive Disclosure and Query Depth

Sprint 2 introduces a more sophisticated challenge: building systems that answer questions at multiple levels of detail. Sometimes a user needs a brief summary. Sometimes they need a detailed breakdown. Sometimes they need the exact source data. You will learn how to prompt your AI system to produce output that can be progressively disclosed — start with a headline, drill down to the evidence, drill down further to the data. This requires more careful specification of what the AI should produce and what constraints should apply at each level.

Sprint 2 is harder

Sprint 1 was a proof of concept. You built a simple component and verified it works. Sprint 2 extends it to handle edge cases, integrate with other tracks' output, and meet production quality standards. The acceptance criteria should be tighter than Sprint 1. The integration tests should verify that your output works when fed into other teams' systems.

3

Sprint 2 Implementation Begins

After contract review and the advanced techniques session, you begin implementation. You know the scope, you know the acceptance criteria, and you have a more sophisticated toolkit for directing AI. Start with your plan from the contract. Break implementation into tasks. Load your context file and begin work.

By end of Tuesday

Sprint 2 contracts approved or revised. Advanced techniques session completed. First implementation tasks underway.

Thursday · Day 12 · Build

Sprint 2 Build

You have been working on Sprint 2 since Tuesday. Thursday is the mid-sprint checkpoint. You have completed some tasks and encountered obstacles on others. You have discovered integration points that work and integration points that need rework. You are also starting to see the differences between Sprint 1 and Sprint 2 process — what worked quickly in Sprint 1 now requires more careful specification in Sprint 2.

1

Continue Implementation

You have two more days to reach the finish line. Focus on integration points — does your component work with data from other tracks? If Track 1 produces a dataset in a particular format, does your Track 2 component correctly consume it? If your Track 2 output feeds into Track 3, does Track 3's component accept the format you produce? Integration failures are often the first real bugs that expose design problems.

2

Methodology Log Update

Continue documenting your process. What advanced techniques worked? Where did progressive disclosure help or hurt? What assumptions did you make about other tracks' output, and were those assumptions correct? If you had to revise your contract mid-sprint, why did that become necessary? The Sprint 2 methodology log should show the difference between how you approached Sprint 1 and how you approach Sprint 2.

Integration is where the system fails

Single components often work well in isolation. The real test comes when multiple components are combined. Thursday is your chance to discover integration failures while you still have time to fix them.

By end of Thursday

Sprint 2 progress checkpoint. Integration points tested. Methodology log updated. Full Sprint 2 due end of Week 7.
