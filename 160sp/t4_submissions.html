How submissions are recorded

Submissions are recorded automatically when you open a Pull Request on GitHub from your track4/<your-name> branch into dkirsh:main. The PR creation timestamp becomes the “Submitted at” entry; the grade and critique link appear after the instructor finishes reviewing.

Team-grade component. Each task carries a small team-grade component (10–15%) that scores the collective-review milestone for that week. Your team submits a single PR containing the milestone notes; the team grade applies to all four students. The bulk of each task's grade remains individual.

What “graded” means here

Each task is graded against the rubric on its own page. Numerical scores reflect the rubric line items; the comments/critique link points at the PR conversation where the instructor records what worked, what needs revision, and what the work taught us about journey design as a practice.
