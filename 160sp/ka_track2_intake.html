Track 2 — Article Intake

This is the student intake surface for Track 2 article collection. It is PDF-first: start by uploading the papers you have already found on your assigned research front. Duplicate detection runs server-side during staging (hash, DOI, fuzzy title + authors). If you are signed in, your submissions are attributed to your student record and count toward your Track 2 metrics; if you are not signed in they are recorded anonymously — that attribution is the difference between this page and the public propose-an-article form.

Rules at a glance
Five gates every Track 2 submission passes through
Immediate gate — duplicate detection As soon as a PDF is inspected, KA computes a SHA-256 hash and cross-checks the corpus by hash, DOI, and fuzzy title + authors. If the paper is already in KA, the upload is stopped and no second copy is stored.
Security gate — untrusted files Public uploads are untrusted until validated. KA verifies PDF magic bytes, rejects encrypted or malformed files, writes to a quarantine directory, and only promotes validated PDFs into the main corpus through an isolated worker.
Track 2 rule — no orphan PDFs Every staged PDF must carry at least first author + title, or DOI + title, so that abstract recovery, duplicate detection, and later repair are straightforward. Use Step 3 below to paste a citation if the automatic extraction is weak.
Deferred gate — relevance review Relevance to your research front is decided asynchronously in the nightly intake run. Students move quickly; maintainers make the harder inclusion decisions in batch.
Storage rule — rejected PDFs are deleted Files that fail validation or the nightly review are purged promptly; only metadata and the rejection reason remain in the audit trail.

Step 3 · Citation-only submission (separate path)
Paste citations, DOIs, or an exported list
This is a separate submission path, not an attachment to your pending PDF batch. Use it when you have DOIs or APA references before you have PDFs — KA will stage them, resolve abstracts, and ask you for the PDFs later. Most Track 2 weeks start with Step 1 (PDFs) instead. If your PDFs in Step 1 came out with wrong metadata, fix that by re-uploading with a corrected filename rather than pasting here.
