## What the student must produce

1. **An interactive scene** in Unity implementing one specific experience that is meant to evoke or test a named T1 framework (e.g., a prospect-refuge choice task; a biophilic exposure scene; a natural-vs-urban soundscape A/B).

3. **A screencast** (2–4 minutes) showing the scene in-headset (or in the Unity editor with headset emulation).
