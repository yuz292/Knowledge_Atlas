# T3.e — User-study pilot (n = 3) with pre/post survey

**Track**: T3
**Span**: Weeks 6–7
**Hardness**: Hard
**Points**: 15
**Verification**: Automated (data-file presence + schema check) + AI re-grade of interpretation writeup

---

## What the student must produce

1. **Three recruited participants** (classmates or lab members, with class-protocol consent).
2. **A pre-post survey** with at least four Likert items per condition, captured as a CSV in `160sp/tracks/t3/{student_id}/study/T3.e_survey.csv`.
3. **A protocol document** (~ 300 words) describing the participant task, the scenes shown, the order, and the survey instrument.
4. **An interpretation writeup** (~ 400 words) summarising the three participants' responses, noting one pattern that emerged, one non-pattern, and what a larger-n study would need to do differently.
