# T3.g — Final demo + reflection

**Track**: T3
**Span**: Week 8
**Hardness**: Easy
**Points**: 10
**Verification**: AI pre-grades writeup; instructor scores the live demo

---

## What the student must produce

1. **A live demo** (~ 8 minutes) at the Week-8 class meeting, including one framework-mapped scene, one interaction walkthrough, and a Q-and-A.
2. **A demo-script document** (~ 500 words) written before the demo, which the student can reference during Q-and-A.
3. **A short reflection** (~ 400 words) after the demo, covering what went well, what did not, and what the student would change about the project arc.
