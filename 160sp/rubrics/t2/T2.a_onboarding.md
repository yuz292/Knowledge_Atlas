# T2.a — Pipeline onboarding + first 15 articles

**Track**: T2 (Article Finder)
**Span**: Week 3
**Hardness**: Easy
**Points**: 5
**Verification**: Automated (registry) + AI re-grade on relevance + reflection

---

## What the student must produce

1. **Environment check**: successful login to the article-submission frontend (`ka_contribute.html`), confirmed by at least one test-submission accepted into the registry.
2. **Declared sub-topic**: a one-paragraph declaration (~ 120 words) naming the sub-topic the student intends to cover for the rest of the track, with one sentence of motivation and two candidate framework tags (from the T1 set of 11).
3. **Fifteen articles** submitted via `ka_contribute.html` with full metadata (PDF, DOI, citation, abstract pasted, framework tag, VOI band).

```yaml

  scoring_method: three_sub_check
  specificity_threshold_for_score_2: 2
  specificity_threshold_for_score_3: 3
```

## Bands

Standard three-criterion bands. Completeness gated on row count + metadata completeness; Quality on the 5-article spot-check; Reflection on the sub-topic declaration's specificity.
