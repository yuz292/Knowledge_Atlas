# T2.g — Final report + reflection

**Track**: T2
**Span**: Week 8
**Hardness**: Easy
**Points**: 5
**Verification**: AI primary + instructor reads every final report

---

## What the student must produce

A 1,200–1,800-word final report in `160sp/tracks/t2/{student_id}/final_report.md` structured as:

1. **Executive summary** (~ 150 words) — sub-topic, final article count, three headline findings.
2. **Method** (~ 250 words) — search strategy, inclusion/exclusion, VOI-banding, dedup.
3. **Coverage** (~ 300 words) — what's covered, what's thin, one figure showing sub-theme distribution.
4. **Findings** (~ 400 words) — three substantive claims the 150-article set supports.
5. **Reflection** (~ 300 words) — what was learned about searching and curating literature.
6. **References** (APA) — minimum 6 references.
