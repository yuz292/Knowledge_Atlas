# T2.c — VOI-banding calibration against 20-article gold set

**Track**: T2
**Span**: Week 5
**Hardness**: Medium-hard
**Points**: 10
**Verification**: Automated (band comparison) + AI re-derivation of VOI bands

---

## What the student must produce

1. **VOI-band assignments** on a 20-article gold set (articles pre-scored by the instructor or track lead), submitted as a markdown table in the shared doc.
2. A **calibration report** (200–300 words) identifying disagreements between the student's bands and the gold-set bands, with a one-sentence explanation per disagreement.
