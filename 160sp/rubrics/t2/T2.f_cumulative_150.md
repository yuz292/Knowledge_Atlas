# T2.f — 150-article cumulative target + dedup + topic-coverage audit

**Track**: T2
**Span**: Weeks 7–8
**Hardness**: Hard
**Points**: 15
**Verification**: Automated (registry aggregation) + AI re-grade of coverage audit writeup

---

## What the student must produce

1. **Cumulative 150 articles** across all T2 deliverables, all on the student's sub-topic, all deduplicated, all with full metadata and VOI bands.
2. **A topic-coverage audit** (400–600 words) comparing the student's 150-article coverage against a target coverage profile (either from a prior systematic review in the field, or derived by the student from 3+ review articles).
3. **A release note** (~ 200 words) describing what the 150-article set is good for and where it is thin.
