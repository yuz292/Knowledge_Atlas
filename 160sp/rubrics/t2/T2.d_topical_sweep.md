# T2.d — 50-article topical-coverage sweep (one sub-topic)

**Track**: T2
**Span**: Weeks 5–6
**Hardness**: Hard
**Points**: 15
**Verification**: Automated (registry count + topical coverage analysis) + AI re-grade of coverage rationale

---

## What the student must produce

1. **Fifty articles** on a single sub-topic within the student's declared area, chosen to cover the sub-topic systematically rather than opportunistically.
2. **A coverage map** (markdown table or small diagram) showing how the 50 articles partition the sub-topic into ~ 5–10 sub-themes, with article counts per theme.
3. **A coverage rationale** (~ 400 words) explaining why this partition is defensible, citing at least two prior review articles on the same sub-topic.
