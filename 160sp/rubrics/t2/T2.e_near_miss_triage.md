# T2.e — Near-miss triage: adjudicate 30 near-miss cases

**Track**: T2
**Span**: Week 6
**Hardness**: Medium
**Points**: 10
**Verification**: Automated (near_miss table) + AI re-grade of adjudications

---

## What the student must produce

1. Pull 30 near-miss cases from `pipeline_registry_unified.near_miss` (articles the classifier flagged as borderline on the student's sub-topic).
2. For each near-miss, record one of three adjudications: `accept` (keep in corpus), `reject` (remove from corpus), `route_to_topic_expansion_candidate` (flag the classifier may need a new topic).
3. **For each decision, a one-sentence justification** in a markdown table.
