# T4.g — Final report + reflection

**Track**: T4
**Span**: Week 8
**Hardness**: Easy
**Points**: 5
**Verification**: AI primary + instructor reads every final report

---

## What the student must produce

A 1,200–1,800-word final report at `160sp/tracks/t4/{student_id}/final_report.md` structured as:

1. **Executive summary** (~ 150 words).
2. **Method** (~ 250 words) — heuristic audit, scenarios, pilot, severity rubric, reproducibility.
3. **Findings** (~ 400 words) — top 5 with severity; backlog summary.
4. **Redesign** (~ 300 words) — summary of the proposal.
5. **Reflection** (~ 300 words).
6. **References** (APA) — minimum 6 references, including Nielsen (1994), Rubin & Chisnell (2008), and at least one paper from the T1 framework pages' classic-5 lists.
