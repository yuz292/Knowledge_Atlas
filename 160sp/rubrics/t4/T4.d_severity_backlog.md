# T4.d — Friction-point severity rubric + prioritised backlog

**Track**: T4
**Span**: Week 6
**Hardness**: Medium-hard
**Points**: 10

---

## What the student must produce

1. **A severity rubric document** (~ 400 words) defining 1–5 severity levels with examples drawn from the student's own T4.a and T4.c findings, following Nielsen (1994) and refined with the impact dimensions the student observed.
2. **A prioritised backlog CSV** at `T4.d_backlog.csv` with all findings from T4.a and T4.c, scored on severity + frequency + scope; rank-ordered.
3. **A backlog-narrative document** (~ 300 words) explaining the top 3 items and the bottom 3 items.
