# T4.f — Redesign proposal for one high-severity finding

**Track**: T4
**Span**: Weeks 7–8
**Hardness**: Medium
**Points**: 10
**Verification**: Automated (file presence) + AI re-grade of proposal quality + instructor audit on feasibility

---

## What the student must produce

1. **A redesign proposal document** (~ 700 words) for one of the student's top-5 findings (by backlog rank). Structure: current state (with screenshot), proposed state (with wireframe or annotated mock), interaction narrative, expected-impact claim with a testable prediction, implementation-cost estimate.
2. **A wireframe or annotated mock** (PNG or PDF) at `160sp/tracks/t4/{student_id}/T4.f_wireframe.png`.
3. **A testable-prediction statement**: "If we ship this redesign, friction point X will be resolved; specifically, task completion rate for {role} on {scenario} will increase by at least {N %} in a follow-up pilot."
