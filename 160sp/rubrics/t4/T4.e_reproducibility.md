# T4.e — Reproducibility check: second student confirms 5 findings

**Track**: T4
**Span**: Week 7
**Hardness**: Hard
**Points**: 13
**Verification**: Automated (cross-reference to partner's findings) + AI re-grade of confirmation writeup

---

## What the student must produce

1. **A paired student** (another T4 student, assigned at Week 3) attempts to reproduce 5 of this student's T4.a or T4.c findings, given only the scenario and page, without seeing the finding text.
2. **A confirmation report** (~ 400 words) in `160sp/tracks/t4/{student_id}/T4.e_confirmation.md` listing each of the 5 findings, whether the partner reproduced it, and a one-paragraph per-finding discussion of convergences and divergences.
3. **The partner's independent findings files** at `T4.e_partner/*.md` (structured as T4.a findings).
