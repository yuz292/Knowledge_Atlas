# T1.d — HITL validation on 50-image confusing-cases batch

**Track**: T1 (Image Tagging)
**Span**: Weeks 4–5
**Hardness**: Medium
**Points**: 10
**Verification**: Automated (DB) + AI re-grade of HITL rationales

---

## What the student must produce

1. Pull the 50-image "confusing cases" batch from the tag-DB (images where any prior tagger's confidence < 0.6 or kappa disagreement ≥ 1).
2. Re-tag each image with: final tag, 0–1 confidence, a **structured rationale** with three fields — `schema_feature_observed`, `competing_framework_considered`, `decision_rule_applied`.
3. Produce a 200–300-word HITL-log entry noting three cases where the competing framework was genuinely defensible.

## Scoring bands

### Completeness

- **0** — < 20 images re-tagged.
- **1** — 20–39 re-tagged.
- **2** — 40–49 re-tagged with structured rationale + HITL log.
- **3** — 50 re-tagged, structured rationale complete, HITL log meets spec.

### Quality

- **0** — Rationales are generic or missing fields.
- **1** — Schema-feature observed is named but competing framework and decision rule are vague.
- **2** — All three fields populated on ≥ 80 % of images.
- **3** — All three fields populated on all images, and competing frameworks cited are genuinely plausible (not strawmen).

### Reflection

Standard bands.
