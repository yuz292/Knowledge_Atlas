# T1.b — Tag 100 images against the full schema

**Track**: T1 (Image Tagging)
**Span**: Weeks 3–4
**Hardness**: Medium
**Points**: 10
**Verification**: Automated (Atlas tag DB) + spot-check on 10 images
**Rubric authored**: 2026-04-17

---

## What the student must produce

1. **One hundred images** tagged against the full schema, each with: tag or tags, 0–1 confidence score per tag, one-sentence rationale, and a flag for any image the student found ambiguous.
2. A short tag-log entry (~ 200 words) identifying the two or three image types (e.g., "open-plan offices with partial daylight") that were hardest to tag consistently, and a proposal for how to split or merge schema categories to improve future tagging.

## How it is scored

Raw 0–9 → rescaled to 10 points. One point of the total is the timeliness bonus.

### Completeness

- **0** — < 40 images tagged.
- **1** — 40–79 tagged.
- **2** — 80–99 tagged.
- **3** — 100 tagged, plus the tag-log entry.

### Quality

- **0** — Spot-check agrees on < 40 % of the TA's re-tags.
- **1** — Spot-check agrees on 40–59 %.
- **2** — Spot-check agrees on 60–79 % (the pre-kappa target).
- **3** — Spot-check agrees on 80 %+, confidence scores are well-distributed (not all 1.0), rationales are specific to image features, and ambiguity flags are used.

### Reflection

- **0** — No tag-log entry.
- **1** — A tag-log entry that notes difficulty but does not propose a schema change.
- **2** — A tag-log entry that names two or three difficult image types with examples.
- **3** — A tag-log entry that proposes a schema split/merge with a defensible rationale, citing at least two specific images from the 100-image batch.

## Verification procedure (TA)

1. Automated row count: `SELECT count(*) FROM atlas_tags WHERE tagged_by = ? AND tagged_at BETWEEN ? AND ?` → must be ≥ 100 for full Completeness.
2. Spot-check: pull 10 random rows and independently re-tag those images, then compute agreement rate. This is not yet a formal kappa (that's T1.c); it is a sanity check.
3. Read the tag-log entry against the Reflection band.

Estimated grading time per student: 20–25 minutes (the spot-check re-tagging dominates).

## Late / missing

Late → − 1 per day late, floored at 0 after 5 days. A student who has not completed T1.b by Week 5 Monday cannot meaningfully attempt T1.c (which requires a 100-image base) and should be placed on a track-lead recovery plan.
