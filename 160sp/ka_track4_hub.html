Total Track 4 allocation: 75 points (10 of which are claimed by T4.b.2, the topic-page variant evaluation). The full class is 100 points with the standard 5/5/75/15 split; Track 4 fills the 75-point project block.

Track 4 owns the redesign lifecycle on the twelve complicated surfaces. The Atlas has twelve "complicated surfaces" — epistemic network, Bayesian network, interpretation, argumentation, QA, mechanism, theory, gaps, evidence landscape, research fronts, ontology inspector, topic inspector — that each publish a spec page describing what the page shows, who needs it, why the design is hard, the naive solution in place now, and a per-user-type critique prompt. Every other track can read these pages as a reference resource; only Track 4 proposes redesigns. The seven graded deliverables above are the pipeline: T4.a audits find the problems, T4.b walks through user scenarios on them, T4.c pilots them, T4.d prioritises the findings, T4.e checks reproducibility, T4.f delivers three mid-fidelity redesigns per student, T4.g reports out. Pages still at naive or absent status carry the highest redesign leverage. Browse the complicated-surfaces index →

Scope note. The three Article Finder spec pages (ka_journey_af_references, ka_journey_af_roi, ka_journey_af_neuro) share the same journey index but belong to Track 2 as graded deliverables T2.d, T2.e, and T2.f. Track 2 students tackle them after their automation pipeline (T2.a citation harvest, T2.b abstract screening, T2.c PDF acquisition) passes its functional checks. A Track 4 student may critique an AF spec's explanation quality as part of T4.a, but the implementation itself stays with Track 2.

Region 4
Task context & manifests
UX-001 · Heuristic audit of a single page Well-specified
Goal
Produce a written heuristic audit of one named page.
Outputs
Markdown report with at least 6 findings, each tagged with a Nielsen heuristic, a severity (0–4), and a concrete remediation.
Acceptance
Instructor accepts report; at least 3 remediations are shipped as PRs.
Verification
Procedural: report file exists, schema conforms, shipped PRs link back to findings.
UX-002 · WCAG 2.1 AA contrast sweep Well-specified
Goal
Identify every text/background pair in the site below 4.5:1 contrast and propose compliant replacements.
Verification
Automated: verify_contrast.py runs axe against all site pages and reports zero regressions after fixes.
UX-003 · Keyboard-only navigation pass Partially specified
Goal
Drive the entire site with keyboard only; identify every unreachable interactive element.
Verification
Hybrid: script checks for tabindex and focus-visible; human confirms flow on at least three representative user journeys.
Region 5
Functional verification

UX deliverables are harder to auto-grade than VR or Article Finder, so we combine automated checks (axe, Lighthouse, contrast) with human sign-off on a scripted user journey.

  • Lighthouse Accessibility score must not decrease after any merge.
  • axe must report zero critical issues on pages changed in the PR.
  • Journey scripts (list of step descriptions) must be reproducible by a second student in < 5 minutes.
