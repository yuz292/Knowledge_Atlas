User Experience

Shape how visitors navigate, read, and trust the Knowledge Atlas. Your deliverables are interfaces, information architectures, and heuristic-driven critiques that improve the site's usability for students, researchers, theorists, and practitioners.

Spring 2026 roster: 4 enrolled students

Region 1
Environment setup

  • Node 18+ and a local clone of the Knowledge_Atlas repo
  • axe DevTools or Lighthouse extension for accessibility audits
  • Figma account (free tier sufficient) for wireframes

Region 3
Ways of working
  • Solo or pair-claim a UX task; deliver via Pull Request.
  • Every heuristic critique cites the specific heuristic violated and a fix.
  • Keyboard navigability and colour contrast are non-negotiable on any merge.
Region 4
Task context & manifests
UX-001 · Heuristic audit of a single page Well-specified
Goal
Produce a written heuristic audit of one named page.
Outputs
Markdown report with at least 6 findings, each tagged with a Nielsen heuristic, a severity (0–4), and a concrete remediation.
Acceptance
Instructor accepts report; at least 3 remediations are shipped as PRs.
Verification
Procedural: report file exists, schema conforms, shipped PRs link back to findings.
UX-002 · WCAG 2.1 AA contrast sweep Well-specified
Goal
Identify every text/background pair in the site below 4.5:1 contrast and propose compliant replacements.
Verification
Automated: verify_contrast.py runs axe against all site pages and reports zero regressions after fixes.
UX-003 · Keyboard-only navigation pass Partially specified
Goal
Drive the entire site with keyboard only; identify every unreachable interactive element.
Verification
Hybrid: script checks for tabindex and focus-visible; human confirms flow on at least three representative user journeys.
Region 5
Functional verification

UX deliverables are harder to auto-grade than VR or Article Finder, so we combine automated checks (axe, Lighthouse, contrast) with human sign-off on a scripted user journey.

  • Lighthouse Accessibility score must not decrease after any merge.
  • axe must report zero critical issues on pages changed in the PR.
  • Journey scripts (list of step descriptions) must be reproducible by a second student in < 5 minutes.
