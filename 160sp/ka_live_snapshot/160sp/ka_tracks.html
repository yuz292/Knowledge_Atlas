Choose Your Track

Now that you've completed the article collection assignment, spend the rest of the quarter on one of four specialized tracks. Each track builds different skills while contributing to the Knowledge Atlas.

Track ONE: Image Tagger

Build the visual annotation pipeline for the Knowledge Atlas — collect 500 images, write detectors for six latent tags, run them at scale, and ship a public viewer. Three tasks across the quarter.

Track TWO: Article Finder

Build a PRISMA-compliant article discovery pipeline — fix the contribute page, extract knowledge gaps, generate targeted queries, execute searches, and triage results at the abstract level.
