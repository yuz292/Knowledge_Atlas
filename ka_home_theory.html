ALERT

Underdetermination Alert

When multiple theories equally explain the data, we mark it explicitly. See which theories are observationally equivalent and what critical tests could break the tie.

⚠️
Observational Equivalence Detected
For Nature × Attention Recovery: Affective Restoration Theory (ART) and Stress Regulation Theory (SRT) are observationally equivalent across 8 of 14 studies. Only 3 studies measure sufficient mechanism-level variables to discriminate.

Current coherence: ART = 0.72, SRT = 0.68 (difference: 0.04). This marginal advantage reflects background fit and simplicity, not empirical discrimination.

Resolution timeline: Critical test feasible in 12–18 months. 3 labs currently planning intervention studies.
