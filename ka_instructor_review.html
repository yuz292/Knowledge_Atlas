Manual Password Reset

Use this when a student cannot receive reset email. Enter the registered account email, generate a one-time reset link, then send that link yourself after you verify the student's identity.

If the student's mail is down, you may send this link manually by a channel you already trust, such as a verified phone number from course records. The system does not send SMS by itself.
