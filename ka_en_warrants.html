The Evidence Number (EN) is not a single judgment. It is the combination of seven typed warrants, each contributing a credibility weight (d) between 0.25 and 1.0. The weights below describe what each warrant adds (or subtracts) for this specific article. The full warrant taxonomy is documented in contracts/web_of_belief.md; the per-article values are persisted at pipeline stage 17 (warrant_assignment).
