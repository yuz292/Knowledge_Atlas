About Annotation Types

CALIBRATION_NOTE
Credence flagged for manual review — too high or too low relative to evidential base
SENSITIVITY_FLAG
Belief credence is highly sensitive to network topology changes
REPLICATION_STATUS
Cross-paper replication count for this finding
SURPRISE_FLAG
Credence significantly above or below its theory's mean
DISPUTE
Contradicted by one or more other beliefs in the web
CROSS_DOMAIN
Belief bridges multiple theories — high interdisciplinary relevance

MECHANISM_GAP — A mechanistic link is hypothesized but lacks direct evidence
SCOPE_LIMITATION — Finding is population- or context-specific; generalization risk
THEORY_CONFLICT — Finding is inconsistent with predictions of a named framework theory
