Page purpose (for workflow and interface designers): This is the article discovery page. It is distinct from the Signal / Measuring Instrument Catalogue (which explains what instruments measure and how to choose them) and from the Evidence Browser (which shows processed evidence nodes with warrant chains). This page is for students and researchers who want to find the primary literature: which studies used EDA [Electrodermal Activity] to measure stress, which studies used EEG [Electroencephalography] and PVT [Psychomotor Vigilance Task] together for sustained attention, and so on. The three search modes (by construct, by instrument, by both) implement progressive specificity: a student exploring a new construct starts broad; a student who has already chosen their instruments searches by both. The results link to full article records, abstract, DOI [Digital Object Identifier], and (when available) the extracted evidence nodes in the Evidence Browser.
